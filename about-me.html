About Me

Hello! My name is Jordan. I am originally from New York, but I've lived in Seattle for almost 15 years. I went to school for graphic design and did that professionally in NYC for a couple years and then moved to Seattle to pursue a career in health care. For the last 15 years I've worked at the University of Washington Medical Center Montlake.

For the first 12 years I worked in the patient care side of the medical center. At first I provided direct patient care as a Patient Care Technician (CNA) and patient transport, but then after a few years I moved into a supervisory role (Patient Transport Supervisor) where I co-supervised 60 employees and also did scheduling and time keeping.

For the last 3 years I've worked for the Design and Construction Management department where assist project managers with facilitating construction projects big and small throughout the medical center. I also project manage some of the smaller construction projects and requests.

In February 2024 I started a certificate in Front-end Development course in an effort to explore the world of tech as a possible career change.
